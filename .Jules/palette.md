## 2025-05-18 - Added Accessibility Labels to Profile Scanner Close Button\n**Learning:** Icon-only close buttons in modals are easily missed for accessibility. The `ProfileScanner` had a custom close button missing `accessibilityRole` and `accessibilityLabel`, which makes it invisible/confusing to screen readers.\n**Action:** Always ensure that custom icon-only buttons (especially Close/Dismiss actions in modals/overlays) have `accessibilityRole="button"` and an `accessibilityLabel` explaining the action.
